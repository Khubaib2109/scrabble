How to use this tracker

Board scoring: click a square and type letters, or use the “Place word” form. Pending tiles are green. When the preview looks right, click Commit scored move.

Premium squares: letter/word multipliers apply only to newly placed tiles. Once a move is committed, those bonuses are consumed, just like physical Scrabble.

Scoring logic: the app scores the main word, any cross-words created by new tiles, and adds the 50-point bingo bonus for 7+ new tiles. It does not validate dictionary legality, challenges, tile bag limits, or end-game rack deductions.

Workbook import: I imported the players and existing totals from your spreadsheet. Because the spreadsheet does not store board coordinates, the board starts empty even when workbook totals are loaded.
